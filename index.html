How It Works

Transform your documents in just three simple steps

1

Upload Your Document

Start by uploading your PDF, Word document, or text file. You can drag and drop, browse your files, or paste a URL to a document online. Our system supports all major document formats with secure processing.

2

Customize Your Output

Choose your preferred Q&A format - detailed explanations, concise flashcards, or multiple choice questions. Select the complexity level and specify any particular focus areas you want emphasized in the generated questions.

3

Download & Share

Download your customized Q&A set as a PDF or editable Word document. Share with classmates via email or social media, or generate a private link for collaborative studying. All your generated sets are saved for future access.
